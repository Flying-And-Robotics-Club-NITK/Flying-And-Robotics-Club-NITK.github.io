DIGIMEDIA

WHAT DO WE DO?

Digimedia, or the Media team of FARC, is a Creative and Content-driven Team. It is primarily known for Farchives or the digital representation of news, new technology or any Important Work that has been achieved by the world in the Flying and Robotics Department. The team also handles Media posts for Social platforms like Facebook, Instagram, and LinkedIn which primarily consists of a graphic or a video which is based usually on a famous festival, important Indian dates or any of Important World dates.

The team was started in July of 2019 as a separate group within FARC and started innovating in the creative department by the third week of August. We are an emerging team in this college which is mostly privileged by separate media sigs in Clubs and we are happy to share that we have lasted an impression by some of our great work in the past few months. We are a team who learn when we are not able, who work when the days are less and thrive to innovate in the media and content department of the club, we are the media and marketing team also known as Digimedia
