ROBOTORQUE



ROBOTIC ARM

The Robotic Arm is a five degrees of freedom manipulator. It was designed using Fusion360. It has been manufactured using laser cutting technology from Acrylic sheet. The arm was simulated in VRep. The arm was designed to be controlled by the simulation over a wireless network using ROS.

AEROWING



ORNITHOPTER

There has been an increasing interest in the development of MAV's (Micro-Air Vehicles) which are smaller than similar aircraft's, designed and produced by using state of the art technology. Among the various types of MAV's that have been developed over the years, the ornithopter is considered as one of most ingenious technology developed; a machine designed to achieve flight by flapping wings like a real bird. The idea of this project was to create an ornithopter from scratch, to control it remotely, and of course to make it fly.
