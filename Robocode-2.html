ROBOCODE

WHAT DO WE DO?

Robocode is a special interest group of Flying and Robotics Club dedicated to developing innovating computer science solutions. They speicalize in fields ranging from Web Development to machine learning.

In adherence to the programming languages and algorithms we master, our projects maily involve developing solutions to machine learning and artifical Intelligence problems. Besides this, a part of our team is dedicated to web development with an aim to developing more efficent, structured and creative front-end and back-end solutions. With a all-round skilled team of programmers, learning and applying happens simultaneously which pushes everyone to new limits and raises their tags in any language they aim to perfect.
