VISIONNAIRE

RECREATE | INNOVATE

As students of engineering, we have generally been confined to the 4 walls of a classroom with scant opportunities to put our knowledge to the test. One of the main drawbacks that we have after leaving the institution is the disconnect between what we have learned in class and the work assigned to us.

Visionnaire, brought to you by FARC, in association with IE-NITK and SAE-NITK, hopes to help you in overcoming this problem with the 'Industry Defined Problem' or IDP. Industry Defined Problem is a platform for all the budding engineers out there to test their mettle by solving a real problem that occurs in the industry, provided to you by industry leaders. Here, you will get to show your skills in your field away from the confines of a classroom. It consists of 2 rounds - preliminary, wherein all submissions to the problem statement are reviewed and the best chosen, and the finals.

FAQ


Who is eligible to apply?
Students enrolled in any college from any Department are welcome to apply.
How many team members?
3 members in a team are required to apply.
Is there a registration fee?

No. Registrations are completely free of cost.

Will accomodation be provided?

Unfortunately, we won't be able to provide any accommodation to students.

Can team members be from different branches?
Yes, team members can be of different branches.
Can you be a part of multiple problem statements?
Yes you can take up multiple problem statements in Prelims. Though if you qualify for the finals you can only take ONE problem statement.
How many teams get selected for the finals?

We are planning to select 6-8 teams across 4 problem statements for the final.

Is a working model required?
No, a simulation will be sufficient (if required).
