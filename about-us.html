What Do We Do?

Flying And Robotics Club (FARC), NITK is a technical club of motivated people who thrive to achieve excellence in Aeronautics and Robotics technology. Since its inception, in campus, the club has made its presence felt.

FARC has five SIGs (Student Integers Groups) :
1.   RoboCode
2.  RoboTorque
3.  RoboTronix
4.  AeroWing
5.  DigiMedia

FARC conducts flight demonstrations in the campus and caters to the needs of flying enthusiasts in the institute. ENGINEER witnesses one of the biggest FARC events ‘WRIGHT FLIGHT’ which attracts participants from different colleges.

Within the club, we discuss future prospects in technology, develop organizational and leadership skills by working with like minded people to the best of our abilities.
