MEET THE TEAM - Year 2019


My journey at FARC, NITK has been extraordinary. Meeting the Flying and Robotics enthusiasts from different fields and working with them was a phenomenal experience. From Wright Brother's plane to Boeing 777x we have come a long way in the field of flying and our members have learned the art of manufacturing and piloting the planes. Robotics is an interdisciplinary branch that includes all fields of engineering and FARC is a perfect example of how different fields can collaborate and work together. The goal of FARC is to design intelligent machines that can help and assist humans in their day-to-day lives and keep everyone safe. To keep the members at the forefront of technology, the club has been inculcating the habit of including challenging projects and I encourage the members of the club to continue the same in the future.


Nishant Kumar

Convenor, 2019-20

My journey started from being a recruit in the first year to the Joint Convenor of the club in my final year, it has been a marvellous and thrilling journey. I will miss the Club meetings, project reviews and random meeting with the club members. I will cherish all memories that I've made in the club for life that I have made in the club. Wishing the club all the success for the future. Best of luck!
