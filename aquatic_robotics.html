Aquatic Technology

THE IDEA

Planet Earth is a breathing marvel and there is still a large fraction of the planet that has not been probed for its treasures. There are thousands of new species being discovered by scientists every year. More than 80% of the oceans remain unchartered till today.

Given the harsh conditions as one dives deeper into the ocean, it ainâ€™t possible for humans to explore the depths of the ocean. With advancing technology, ocean bots have helped overcome this hurdle and this project is aimed at designing our own version of this incredible machine.

This projects aims to design a bio-inspired fish robot which can be modified for special applications. With basic aquatic movements, it would be an Eel inspired fish robot which would maneuver using the Anguilliform propulsion mechanism. The potential applications are Aquatic Environment Survey and Ocean Floor Mapping.

The scope of this project includes creating models aiding in searching for the targets, detecting odour sources, finding the ore veins in the wild field, foraging and rescuing the victims in disaster area and cartography.

  • 1

    Mapping and exploring an area

    A swarm mapping behaviour capable of partial surveying and performing area coverage to survey unknown environments. This will be achieved by combining sensor data and camera feed analysis, as required.

  • 2

    Target recognition and detection

    A complete swarm intelligence setup of robots involving 3 or more nodes with basic functionalities like swarm motion, obstacle avoidance

  • 1

    Collaborative Transport and Sub-Swarm Development

    A swarm behaviour compatible with collaborative transport as well as rescue operations wherein it is tasked with finding, locating and Sub-Swarm formation will be developed with respect to the task at hand.
