SPECIAL INTEREST GROUPS

Our SIGs make FARC what it is today.
An image of extreme passion and commitment...


ROBOCODE

A body of highly gifted programmers and puzzle addicts that expertise in fields ranging from Development to Artifical Intelligence.

ROBOTRONIX

A conglomeration of circuitry enthusiasts working on ciruit designing, singnal processing and more. They love designing crazy bots which solve real life problems.

ROBOTORQUE

A bunch of machine lovers that either break or make anything they lay their hands on, expertising in automation and robotics, they come up with robots of a whole new level!

AEROWING

A group of flying enthusiasts specialising in aero-modelling and flying. They really know how to keep NITK airspace busy with planes, drones and what not.

DIGIMEDIA

A pack of desiginers, video editors, photographers and developers who make the backbone of the media presence of FARC.
