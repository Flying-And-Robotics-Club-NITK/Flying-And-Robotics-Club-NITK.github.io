STUDENT INTEREST GROUPS

Our SIGs make FARC what it is today.
An image of extreme passion and commitment...


ROBOCODE

A body of highly gifted programmers and puzzle addicts expertising in feilds ranging from Web Development to Artifical Intelligence

ROBOTRONIX

A conglomeration of circuitry enthusiasts working on ciruit designing, singnal processing and design crazy bots which solve real life problems.

ROBOTORQUE

A bunch of machine lovers that either break or make anything they lay their hands on. Expertising in automation and robotics, they come up with robots on a whole another scale!.

AEROWING

A group of flying enthusiasts who you would usually find grazing the sky during a meet. Specialising in aero-modelling and flying, they really know how to keep NITK airspace busy with planes, drones and what not.

DIGIMEDIA

A pack of desiginers, video editors, photographers and developers who make the backbone of the media presence of FARC.

The Cave Exploration Bot

Many caves have toxic gases, loose soil, rocky structure, and hidden groundwater reserves making then unsafe for human entry. This project aims to create a robot for reaching the inaccessible places in caves more efficently and deliver accurate and valuable information regarding the cave.
