ROBOTIC ARM

Description

Motivation:
Robotic Arms, a group of the most popular and in demand robots of this generation, are mechanical arms which perform the same function as a human arm. This project aims to create one of the same but with more depth, detail and efficency than a standard while applying autonomous, sensitive and accurate movement capabilities to it.
