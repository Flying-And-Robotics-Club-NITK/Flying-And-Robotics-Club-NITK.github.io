ROBOTORQUE

WHAT WE DO?

RoboTorque is a conglomeration of students dedicated to engineering fields like Mechanical, Material Science and CIvil. This group specializes in machine building, operating and testing.

Robotorque believes in hands-on experience to harness knowledge and to give its members a cutting edge in practical applications. RoboTorque mainly works on projects involving machine designing, simulations and testing.

OUR PROJECTS



ROBOTIC ARM

The Robotic Arm is a five degrees of freedom manipulator. It was designed using Fusion360. It has been manufactured using laser cutting technology from Acrylic sheet. The arm was simulated in VRep. The arm was designed to be controlled by the simulation over a wireless network using ROS.
