Swarm Intelligence

THE IDEA

A swarm of robots would work on the same principles as an ant colony: each member has a simple set of rules to follow, leading to self-organization and self-sufficiency. The motivation of this project arises from the need for decentralisation of tasks such as mapping and search-and-rescue operation in events of catastrophe for a better, active and faster response.

This project aims to extend its deliverables to a broad timeline so as to provide a platform for studying and analysis of swarm behaviours in different environments and return feedbacks. By developing a base model for the autonomous control system of individual bots, we can study how presence of multi-robots can affect the performance and efficiency in completing the given task.

The problems involved in swarm robotics research can be classified into two classes. One class of the problems is based on the patterns, such as accumulation, cartography, migration, self-organizing grids, deployment of distributed agents and area coverage. Another class of problems focuses on the entities in the environment, e.g. searching for the targets, detecting odour sources, finding the ore veins in wild field, foraging, rescuing the victims in disaster areas, etc.

Besides these problems, the swarm robotics can also be involved into more sophisticated problems, mostly hybrid of these two classes, including cooperative transportation, demining, exploring a planet and navigating in large area.

  • 1

    Mapping and exploring an area

    A swarm mapping behaviour capable of partial surveying and performing area coverage to survey unknown environments. This will be achieved by combining sensor data and camera feed analysis, as required.

  • 2

    Target recognition and detection

    A complete swarm intelligence setup of robots involving 3 or more nodes with basic functionalities like swarm motion, obstacle avoidance

  • 1

    Collaborative Transport and Sub-Swarm Development

    A swarm behaviour compatible with collaborative transport as well as rescue operations wherein it is tasked with finding, locating and Sub-Swarm formation will be developed with respect to the task at hand.
